Core live endpoints

  • /api/healthService health and runtime metadata.
  • /api/versionCurrent backend version identifier.
  • /api/report?address=Master wallet intelligence response.
  • /api/classification?address=Wallet type, confidence, and identity summary.
  • /api/statement?address=Plain-English wallet explanation.
  • /api/activity?address=Activity summary and sampled transaction context.
  • /api/risk?address=Risk flags and structured notes.
